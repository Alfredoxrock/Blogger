Frequently Asked

Can you review my writing?

While I can't provide detailed manuscript reviews, I'm happy to answer specific craft questions or point you toward helpful resources.

Do you accept guest posts?

Occasionally! Guest posts should align with the blog's focus on craft, fiction, or writing life. Please include a brief outline with your inquiry.

How can I suggest a topic?

I love topic suggestions! Use the contact form above and choose "Other" as the subject. The more specific, the better.

Can I republish your content?

Please reach out before republishing. I'm usually happy to grant permission with proper attribution.
